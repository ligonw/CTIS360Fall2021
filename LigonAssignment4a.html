• Carrots

Good Companions

    Peas
    Radishes
    Lettuce
    Chives
    Sage
    Onions
    Leeks
    Cabbage
    Early Potatoes
    Lettuce
    Rosemary
    *Wormwood

Bad Companions

    None

Comments: Onions, Leeks, and *Wormwood repel carrot flies.

• Corn

Good Companions

    Beans
    Cucumbers
    Early Potatoes
    Melons
    Peas
    Pumpkins
    Soybeans
    Squash

Bad Companions

    None

Comments: soybeans deter chinch bugs.

• Cucumbers

Good Companions

    Potatoes (early crop only)
    Beans
    Celery
    Lettuce
    Sweet Corn
    Cabbage
    Sunflowers
    Nasturtiums

Bad Companions

    Keep away from: late potatoes

Comments: Radishes deter cucumber beetles. Cucumbers encourage blight in late potatoes.

• Leeks

Good Companions

    Celery

Bad Companions

    None

• Lettuce

Good Companions

    Carrots
    Onions
    Strawberries
    Beetroot
    Cabbage
    Radishes
    Marigolds

Bad Companions

    Parsley
    Celery
    Keep away from: cabbage family

Comments: lettuce tenderizes summer radishes.

• Onions

Good Companions

    Carrots
    Beetroot
    Lettuce
    Chamomile
    Kohlrabi
    Summer Savory

Bad Companions

    Peas
    Beans

Comments: deters most pests, especially maggots.

• Potatoes

Good Companions

    Peas
    Beans
    Cabbage
    Sweetcorn
    Nasturtiums
    Marigolds
    Foxgloves
    Horse Radish
    Eggplant
    Hemp
    Squash

Bad Companions

    Apples
    Cherries
    Cucumbers
    Pumpkins
    Sunflowers
    Tomatoes
    Raspberries
    Rosemary
    Walnuts

Comments: hemp deters phytophthora infestans. Basil deters potato beetles. Marigolds (dug into crop soil) deter nematodes.
